About Baskin Robbins

Baskin Robbins has been serving the world with 31 flavors of ice cream since 1945. Our mission is to spread smiles and joy through handcrafted ice creams, sundaes, and cakes. We take pride in using high-quality ingredients and offering a delightful experience for every customer.

Our Story

Founded by Burt Baskin and Irv Robbins, we started with one small ice cream shop. Over decades, we have expanded globally and brought innovative flavors, seasonal delights, and festive treats to ice cream lovers everywhere.

Our Values

  • Quality ingredients and craftsmanship
  • Customer happiness and satisfaction
  • Innovation and creativity in flavors
  • Community engagement and responsibility
